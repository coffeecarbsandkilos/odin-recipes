Zuppa Toscana Recipe

Authentic Zuppa Toscana is the perfect cold weather meal!

What You Will Need

  • 6 quart sauce pan or Dutch Oven
  • 1 pound spicy italian sausage
  • 5 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 medium yellow onion diced
  • 1 1/2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes (or similar)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups chopped kale
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • Freshly grated parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Add a small amount of oil to a pan on medium heat and sauté onions until softened.
  2. Add garlic to pan and sautee for another 30 seconds.
  3. Add the sausage to the pan, breaking it up and sauté until cooked through or about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the potatoes, Italian seasoning and chicken broth, and bring it to a boil. then, reduce the heat to medium-low and let it simmer uncovered for about 10 minutes, until the potatoes are fork tender.
  5. Once the potatoes are done, add the chopped kale, bacon, and heavy cream. Give everything a stir and let simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with black pepper and fresh grated parmesan and serve!
